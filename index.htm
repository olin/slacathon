WHAT IS SLACFEST?

SLACfest is Olin College’s first official design, hacking, and making marathon, hosted by Olin’s dedicated Stay Late And Create (SLAC) student organization. SLAC is passionate about providing opportunities for people to come together and fostering new ideas and projects. In the spirit of SLAC, this first SLACfest will be focused on community building.

WHAT IS SLACFEST?

SLACfest is Olin College's first official design, creativity, and making marathon, hosted by Olin's Stay Late And Create (SLAC) student organization. SLAC is passionate about providing opportunities for people to come together and fostering new ideas and projects of all types. In the spirit of SLAC, the purpose of this event is to encourage to the community to come together and do awesome things. Come experience the creativity of a community first-hand!

FAQ

Who can come?


Anyone over the age of 18. Invite your friends! Don't forget to register! No need to stay the whole time. Stay for a couple of hours and do something awesome!

Is this a hackathon?


No, but it can be if you want it to be. It's a place and time for people to do anything that involves creativity! Do something awesome.

What should I bring?


Anything that will help you do something awesome. Please adhere to our code of conduct (in registration)!
